About Us

Tanishq's Cafe has been serving the community since 1990. Our mission is to provide a cozy environment where people can relax, work, and enjoy quality food and beverages.

Our History

Founded by a group of coffee enthusiasts, Tanishq's Cafe started as a small neighborhood cafe and has grown into a beloved establishment known for its delicious coffee and friendly atmosphere.

Over the years, we have expanded our menu to include a variety of sandwiches, pastries, and other treats. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has remained unchanged.

Come and experience the rich history and inviting atmosphere of Tanishq's Cafe We look forward to serving you!
